EmptyByte was founded in 2026 by seven young entrepreneurs who shared a single conviction: the gap between a powerful idea and a powerful digital product should not exist. We didn't set out to become an agency — we set out to solve real problems, elegantly.

Based in Palakkad, Kerala, our founders came together from diverse disciplines — mobile engineering, full-stack development, UI/UX design, business strategy, and systems thinking. Each one brought a unique perspective. Together, we built something greater than the sum of our parts.

FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary by scope. A website takes 3–5 weeks, a mobile app 6–12 weeks, and larger platforms 3–6 months. We always provide a detailed timeline in the proposal before work begins.
What is your pricing model?
We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials models. We're startup-friendly and work with budgets to find the right structure. Every project starts with a transparent, itemised estimate — no surprises.
Do you provide support after launch?
Yes — all projects include a 30-day post-launch support period. We also offer ongoing maintenance retainers for updates, monitoring, and new features as your product grows.
Which industries do you work with?
We've worked across e-commerce, logistics, education, finance, healthcare, and retail. Our technical approach is industry-agnostic — great engineering and design applies everywhere.
Can you work with clients outside Kerala?
Absolutely. We work with clients across India and internationally. Our remote-first workflow ensures seamless collaboration via Slack, Notion, and Figma regardless of location.
How do I get started?
Reach out via the contact form or call us directly. We'll schedule a free discovery call, then send a detailed proposal within 48 hours. No commitment needed until you're ready.

Still have questions?

Reach out directly — we respond to every enquiry within 24 hours. No automated bots, no runaround — just real people who care.
